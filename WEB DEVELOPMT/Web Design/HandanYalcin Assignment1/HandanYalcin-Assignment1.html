PERSONAL INFORMATION

Handan also has a great deal of interest in helping techniques for trauma, NLP, and non-violent communication. She is a certified Family Constellation moderator and trainer. She is currently a student at the Department of Computer Software and Network Engineering Advanced Diploma Program of Sheridan College.
